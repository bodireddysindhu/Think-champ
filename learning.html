ONLINE LEARNING

* Online learning saves us time and means we can learn on the go; so it eliminates the boundaries of the classroom.

* It enables us to easily learn about the world and gain better skills.

* However, this easier access to information means the academic requirements facing students can also be more demanding.

* Online learning may bring a lot of advantages to students.

* They can use e-books, so their schoolbags will be much lighter.
